1. Setup

  • One player creates a lobby and shares the 4-letter code.
  • Other players join using the code and pick nicknames.
  • Wait for everyone, then the host starts the game.

2. Roles & Words

  • Each round, one player is secretly the Spy.
  • Everyone else gets Word A; the Spy gets Word B.
  • Words are similar to make it tricky to spot the Spy!

3. Description Phase

  • Players take turns describing their word with one sentence.
  • If it's your turn, you'll see: "It's your turn to describe!"
  • Be clever‚Äîdon‚Äôt give away too much!

4. Voting Phase

  • Everyone votes who they think the Spy is.
  • If most players vote correctly, Normals win points!
  • If the Spy survives, they score instead!

5. Round Results & Next Round

  • Roles and words are revealed.
  • The host can start a new round anytime.
  • Highest score at the end wins the game!

Tips & Tricks

  • Normals: Be vague, but not suspicious.
  • Spies: Blend in by mimicking other clues.
